The work begins
in the same place
every time.

The underlying conviction remains intact.

Luxury is not addition. It is the removal of everything that does not belong.

III About Pratik

Editorial restraint as
a working method.

Pratik Sengupta directs brand experience at InnStar Limited in Dhaka, a luxury holding company of six practices. His remit covers positioning, naming, editorial direction, and the architecture of how those practices read together.

A mathematics graduate by training and a behavioural-science reader by inclination, his discipline is one of editorial subtraction. The work is restrained, considered, and held to the same register across decades.

Recently: the Bangladesh launch of Jinpeng EV (2025); InnStar Experience Lounge, a luxury residential experience centre directed from brief to opening (2024); Trigon 7 at Dewans, a thirty-storey diagrid tower (in development).

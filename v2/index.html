§ 3The proof, in three

A discipline is not the sum of disciplined practices. It is what holds those practices in tension with each other. Six brands operated as one ensemble; one brochure setting the register for the next; the editorial decision made on a vendor email at midnight, in the same voice as the launch film.

This site is the proof. Three works are presented as three operations. The proposition, restated at the close, is that integration — the holding of disparate parts in editorial tension — is greater than their sum.

Scroll. The argument is short.
